I'm Hannah, a data analyst with a nontraditional background in manufacturing, death care, and real estate. I specialize in using Python, SQL, and Tableau to analyze data and communicate insights that support better decision-making.

Although I'm at the start of my professional analytics career, my work across production, sales, and operations has given me a practical understanding of how businesses operate and where data can make a real impact. My projects focus on answering concrete business questions, with an emphasis on clarity, usability, and storytelling.

Outside of work, I'm passionate about narrative in all its forms, from performance arts to literature to game design. I'm always looking for problems to solve, and I’m especially interested in using data to uncover meaningful patterns and turn them into stories that matter.
